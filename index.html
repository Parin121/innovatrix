How To Play

Controls

  • Left/Right Arrows: Move your character
  • Spacebar: Attack infected enemies

Characters

  • Ellie: Faster movement, quicker attacks
  • Abby: Stronger attacks, more health

Objectives

  • Collect falling tacos to increase your score
  • Regular tacos heal 5 health points
  • Special tacos (glowing) heal 15 health points
  • Find collectibles for bonus points
  • Avoid infected enemies or destroy them

Enemies

  • Runners: Fast but weak
  • Clickers: Moderate speed and strength
  • Bloaters: Slow but tough
  • Rat King: Extremely dangerous boss

Factions

Your actions affect your standing with FEDRA and the Fireflies, changing your game ending!
